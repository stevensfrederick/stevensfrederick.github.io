Three Rotastic Features

Now you know why I like this game and what Roblox is, if you didn't know before.
But are you aware of Roblox's features that I like?

    Dev. Exchange

  1. The Developer Exchange Program (also known as DevEx) allows Robloxians to earn real money by exchanging their Robux from creating awesome (groups, games, etc) that are profitable on Roblox.
    Therefore, my or any Robloxians' game development is like a paid hobby anywhere on the go!

    2. Roblox Toys

  2. Toys based off Robloxian personalities can be purchased at places like Target, Wal-Mart, Best Buy, and more.
    Roblox toy packages are embedded of codes that be can redeemed for in-game virtual items here. It's twice the fun when I and any Robloxians can roleplay with actual Robloxians, from changeable to interchangeable sets of lego-like mini-figures!

    3. Roblox Game Creation System

  3. Roblox is a game creation platform which allows players to create their own games using its proprietary engine, Roblox Studio.
    Games are coded under a object oriented programming system utilizing the programming language, Lua, in order to manipulate the environment of the game.
    This helps me and any Robloxians who wish to power my and their imaginations, while building up their knowledge of coding!
